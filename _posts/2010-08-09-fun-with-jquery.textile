Here is a little jquery script to _webpeerize_ all the images in your page:


{% highlight html %}
<script type="text/javascript" src="neoip_webpeer.js"></script>
<script>
	neoip_webpeer.ready(function(){
		$("img").each(function(){
			this.attr("src", webpeerize(this.attr('src')) );
		})
	});
</script>
{% endhighlight %}

Now your images will be downloaded with _p2p efficiency_. Pretty simple no ?

It would be as simple to webpeerize flash @<object>@ or video @<source>@.
