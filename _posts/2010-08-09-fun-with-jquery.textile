Here is a little jquery script to webpackize all the images in your page:


{% highlight javascript %}
<script type="text/javascript" src="neoip_webpack.js"/>
<script>
	neoip_webpack.ready(function(){
		$("img").each(function(){
			this.attr("src", webpackize(this.attr('src')) );
		})
	});
</script>
{% endhighlight %}

Pretty simple no ? And now all your images will be downloaded with p2p efficiency.

It would be as simple to webpackize flash &lt;object&gt; or video &lt;source&gt; .
