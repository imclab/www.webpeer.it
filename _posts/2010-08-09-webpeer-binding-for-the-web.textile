Here is a little jquery script to _webpeerize_ all the images in your page:


{% highlight html %}
<script type="text/javascript" src="http://webpeer.it/js/webpeer.js"></script>
<script>
	webpeer.ready(function(){
		$("img").each(function(){
			this.attr("src", webpeer.url(this.attr('src')) );
		})
	});
</script>
{% endhighlight %}

Now your images will be downloaded with _p2p efficiency_. Pretty simple no ?
just download <a href="http://webpeer.it/js/webpeer.min.js">webpeer.js</a>.

It would be as simple to webpeerize flash @<object>@ or video @<source>@.
