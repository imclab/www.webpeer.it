Fun with jQuery

09 Aug 2010

Here is a little jquery script to webpeerize all the images in your page:

<script type="text/javascript" src="http://webpeer.it/js/webpeer.js"></script>
<script>
	webpeer.ready(function(){
		$("img").each(function(){
			this.attr("src", webpeer.url(this.attr('src')) );
		})
	});
</script>

Now your images will be downloaded with p2p efficiency. Pretty simple no ?
just download webpeer.js.

It would be as simple to webpeerize flash <object> or video <source>.
