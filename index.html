Webpeer boosts your internet for free. It seamlessly unifies web and p2p to leverage the best both worlds. It is opensource and cross platform. Its carefully crafted design makes it efficient , robust , and transparent . Its deployement is quite flexible. You can choose to be on a desktop , an middlebox or on a server , each providing its own set of advantages.
  • faster downloads for clients
  • more reliable content delivery
  • reduced bandwith for servers
  • no change to your content
  • no change to your software
  • Webpeer is free and opensource. It seamlessly unifies web and p2p to leverage the best both worlds in a breeze. It is efficient , robust and transparent .



  • Efficient

    Webpeer brings p2p efficiency to the web. People in the web peer cloud collaborates with each other. Their sharing allows a reduced server load and a faster downloads for the client.

  • Robust

    Webpeer leverages content from multiple sources. This redundancy allows no single point of failure, that you can see in usual http client/server model. Thus you enjoy more reliable content delivery.

  • Transparent

    Webpeer is transparently integrated to your web environnment without changing it. It has been designed to be non-intrusive. It is transparent for content. Use your unmodified files and streams now! It is transparent for software. Keep using your usual http clients and servers.
